Frequently Asked Questions

How to punch a time card?

To punch a time card, simply insert your time card into the time clock machine and press the punch button. The machine will stamp the current date and time on your card. This process is essential for accurate employee time tracking and attendance management.

What is the best mechanical time clock?

The best mechanical time clock depends on your business needs. Consider factors like durability, ease of use, and maintenance requirements when choosing a time clock for your workplace. For construction sites and manufacturing environments, look for heavy duty time clock options.

How to service a time clock?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the stamp mechanism, replacing time clock ribbons, and ensuring proper alignment. Professional servicing is recommended annually to maintain optimal performance of your time card stamping machine.

Which time clock is best for small business?

For small business, consider a desktop time clock that combines affordability with reliability. Look for features like easy time card management, durable construction, and simple maintenance requirements. Analog time clock options are often cost-effective for small operations.

What are replacement time cards?

Replacement time cards are additional time cards that can be used when original cards are lost or damaged. They maintain the same format and compatibility with your time clock machine, ensuring continuous time tracking operations.
